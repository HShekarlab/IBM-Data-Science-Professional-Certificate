## üéØ Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, learners will be able to:

1. Interpret the key components typically included in a data science job posting.  
2. Explain how data science is applied within the healthcare industry.  
3. Describe how organizations begin their data science transformation.  
4. Identify various ways consumer-generated data is produced and collected.  
5. Explain how leading companies such as Netflix, Amazon, UPS, Google, and Apple leverage data from users and employees.  
6. Compare traits that distinguish data scientists from other data professionals.  
7. Explain the purpose of a final project deliverable and the importance of storytelling in communicating insights.  
8. Describe the structure and essential elements of a comprehensive data science final report.  
9. Demonstrate understanding of the data science workflow and the core components of a professional data science report.
