## üîπ Module Overview
Module 2 focuses on the expanding landscape of **Big Data**, modern computational tools, and the role of intelligent systems in data-driven decision-making.  
Learners examine how large-scale data shapes industries, the technological infrastructure needed to process it, and the analytical techniques behind extracting meaningful insights.  
The module also offers a structured introduction to **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, and their connection to the broader field of Artificial Intelligence.

> This module bridges foundational knowledge with advanced analytical concepts, preparing learners for hands-on work with large datasets and predictive models.

---

## üéØ Learning Objectives
By completing this module, learners will be able to:

1. Define Big Data and differentiate its core characteristics, including scale, speed, and complexity.  
2. Explain how distributed systems and large-scale data platforms contribute to digital transformation.  
3. Identify essential skills required for working with massive datasets.  
4. Describe the fundamental capabilities of cloud computing and why it is critical for Big Data processing.  
5. Explain the concept and workflow of data mining.  
6. Outline key steps in preparing data for mining, including goal-setting, selection, preprocessing, transformation, and storage.  
7. Distinguish between machine learning and deep learning approaches.  
8. Describe regression techniques and how they support forecasting and trend analysis.  
9. Explain the concept of generative AI and its relevance within modern data science.
